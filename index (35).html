TradesConnect
The fastest way for young people in West Michigan to connect with skilled trades programs — no resume, no forms, one tap.
Who we are
TradesConnect is a West Michigan-built platform connecting high school seniors, recent grads, and career changers to local trade programs and apprenticeships. We exist because the application process for trades is broken — most young people never apply because the hoops are too high. We fix that.
How it works
1
Create your profile
Name, location, trades you're interested in. Takes 60 seconds. No resume needed.
2
Browse local programs
See verified apprenticeships and training centers across Kalamazoo and Grand Rapids.
3
Express interest in one tap
Hit the button. Your profile goes straight to the program. They reach out — no formal application yet.
4
Get hired
Connect directly with the program. Start earning while you learn.
